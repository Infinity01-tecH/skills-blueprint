Rare features — nothing like these exists anywhere else
Learning Fingerprint

A 90-second diagnostic before your roadmap generates. Not "beginner or intermediate" — that's lazy. We ask how your brain actually works: do you need the big picture first, or small wins immediately? Do you quit when it gets theoretical? Every roadmap Blueprint ever builds you is shaped around your personal learning fingerprint from that point on.

Rare
The Bottleneck Finder

Blueprint watches your progress in the background. If you skip a step three times, keep it incomplete, or keep revisiting the same phase — the AI surfaces it. "You've avoided CSS Grid four times. Here are three completely different ways to understand it." It doesn't just tell you what to learn. It detects where you're actually breaking and fixes the specific block.

Rare
Skill Crossover

Shows you what happens when two skills combine. "Forex + Psychology + Data Analysis = an edge 95% of traders don't have." Skill multipliers — combinations that create value greater than the sum of their parts. Nobody maps the space between skills. Blueprint does.

Rare
Sprint Blueprint

"I have 14 days. I need to be functional in React for an interview." Give it your deadline and goal — not mastery, just functional. It generates an aggressive, prioritised plan, tells you what to skip, and is honest about the gaps you'll have. Built for career switchers and freelancers who landed a job they need to grow into fast.

Rare
Proof of Work Timeline

Certificates are dead. Nobody believes a Udemy badge. Blueprint generates a living, shareable link — your public learning timeline. Every phase completed, every project built, timestamps, resources used, quiz scores. Send it to a client instead of a CV. They see the receipts, not just the claim.

Rare
The Unlearn List

Every skill has bad habits beginners pick up from bad tutorials that experts spend years fixing. Blueprint shows them before you develop them. "Learning web design? Most tutorials teach desktop-first. You'll spend 6 months unlearning that. Start mobile-first today." Honest in a way no other platform dares to be.
